MuseScore Version of "It's Coming Down Hard" by Alex Haberman

Here is the final version of this track that I wrote in MuseScore before transferring it to Ableton for the sound-development phase. It is structured the same and contains the same amount of instrument parts, but the melody in one of the musical phrases was slighlty altered in Ableton for the published version of the track (aka outside of MuseScore, so the change is not reflected here).

Scrapped Drum Part for the Upcoming Track "Let's Be Real" (Working Title) by Alex Haberman and John Haberman

Here is a rough recording of me playing a drum part that would've been used in a track that my brother John and I still plan to release. We ended up scrapping it in favor of a more syncopated part, since we revamped the rhythm section of the song entirely. There are no plans to incorporate it into any future projects.
