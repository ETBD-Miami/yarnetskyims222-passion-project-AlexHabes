"Untitled Prog Project #1" by Alex Haberman and John Haberman

Here is the progress that my brother John and I have made on a progressive rock tune, which is based on styles from the band King Crimson. It features me on the drum set and him on the electric bass. We're not sure whether we're going to come back to it and flesh it out, but the small amount that we wrote sounds pretty cool (at least in my opinion). It is a rough recording, so expect a lot of mistakes!
